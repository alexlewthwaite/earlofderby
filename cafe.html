Earl of Derby

Mornings start with good coffee. Afternoons are made for lingering.

Overlooking the castle, the Café is where mornings start with a perfectly pulled Girl Crush espresso from Girls Who Grind Coffee, and afternoons are made for lingering — over a well-considered lineup of teas from H.R. Higgins, a glass of wine, or something with a bit more kick. Savoury croissants and bagels keep things simple but satisfying, and the cakes don't tend to hang around for long.
