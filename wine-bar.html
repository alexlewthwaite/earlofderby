Earl of Derby

Slow down, choose well, and enjoy each round.

Set beneath the house, the Wine Bar is an intimate corner of Castletown's evening scene — designed for unhurried nights of good conversation and carefully chosen drinks. World wines, local craft beers and cocktails mixed with a confident, elegant touch, alongside small plates made to share. The kind of place that feels special without trying too hard.
